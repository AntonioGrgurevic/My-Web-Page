My 2 Favourite Artist

Abel Makkonen Tesfaye (born February 16, 1990), known professionally as The Weeknd, is a Canadian singer, songwriter and actor. I started listening to him in 2021. At first I liked a couple songs like Blinding Lights and Save Your Tears. When I got Covid I was lying in my bed and I started listening to entire albums from artists that I listened to at that time. And when I got to listen to every album from The Weeknd I liked a couple more songs around 2-3 from each album and I thought some songs where terrible. But I noticed how diffrent each album was from the prevoius one and that caught me off guard because I thought that every artist had the same sound and feel to every album. I mean thematically every song is either about women and sex or about drugs. But the sound was almost completely diffrent in each album and thats something that even when I wasn't a fan I appriciated. I also loved the stories that he was telling with each song/album. As time went on I started liking more of his songs and albums, and now loving almost every song and album. Even albums that I hated became one of my favourites once I started understanding them and their story. His music helped me through the darkest time of my life and helped me keep my sanity intact. He is also the only artist whose merchandise I bought(I bought a vinyl and a CD).

His albums are: Trilogy(House of Ballons, Thursday, Echoes of Silence), Kiss Land, Beauty Behind the Madness, Starboy, My Dear Melancholy, After Hours, Dawn FM.

Jarad Anthony Higgins (December 2, 1998 - December 8, 2019), known professionally as Juice Wrld, was an American rapper and singer-songwriter.The first song I heard from him was Lucid Dreams and I really liked that song and I still do, but at that time I didn't really stick to one artist I was mostly listening to whatever song sounded good. About a year later I stumbled on a song called Wishing Well. I was pretty deppressed and sad when I first heard that song because I didn't really listen to any sad or deppressing music. I also found what I thought was a compilation at first called Legends Never Die but later I found out that it is acctually an album. It was seriously my first time learning what an album is I always thought an album was just a random compilation of songs. After Wishing Well captivated me I started listening to more songs off the album and I really started liking his songs. I also learned of his passing which made it really hard listening to his songs. His music thematically is about many things some of which are: drugs, deppression, girls, life, friends and so on. I've been listening to his music for about 3 years now and it helped me through many though times. I'm really sad about what happened to him and I hope he finds peace.

His albums are: Goodbye & Good Riddance, WRLD On Drugs, Death Race For Love, Legends Never Die Fighting Demons

My Favourite Albums

1. After Hours

After Hours is an 80's inspired album and it is my favourite album of all time. As I said before it helped me through a really though time in my life and it helped deal with suicidal thoughts I had. The album is near perfect in my eyes(because nothing is perfect) and I had such a great experience first time listening to this album. I can relate to almost every song on the album and I love almost every song. To clarify there are no songs that I dislike there are only some that I don't love. I love the story and theme with each song and I wish that I could listen to it for the first time again. There is also a deluxe version which I also love. I also have the vinyl version of this album. My favourite songs are: Alone Again, Too Late, Heartless, Faith, Blinding Lights, Save Your Tears(it is also my favourite song of all time), In Your Eyes, After Hours, Until I Bleed Out, Nothing Compares(deluxe) and Final Lullaby(deluxe).

2. DawnFM

Dawn FM is a 2. part of After Hours so of course I love it. The theme and story is purgatory. Dawn FM is a radio station that you listen to while travelling purgatory on your way to after life. Dawn FM is even more of an 80's album than it's predecessor. I have fond memories listening to this album at night under a starry sky and while playing League of Legends(LOL). My favourite songs are: Gasoline, How Do I Make You Love Me?, Take My Breath, Sacrifice, Out Of Time, Is There Someone Else?, Starry Eyes, Don't Break My Heart and Less Than Zero.

3. Legends Never Die

Legends Never Die is in my humble opinion the best Juice WRLD album. The vibe and atmosphere are amazing and feel heavenly. It is the first album I ever listened to from front to back and also the first album I cried listening to. It has some bad songs that I never listen to but there are many songs which I really love. My favourite songs are: Conversation, Bad Energy, Righteous, Tell Me U Love Me, Life's A Mess, Come & Go (My top 5 favourite of all time), I Want It, Wishing Well and Man Of The Year.

4. Fighting Demons

Fighting Demons is a weird album. I love some parts of it but I think it should have been made better. There were many problems with the label but I won't get in to it. All and all I have good memories with this album and it helped me like every Juice album. I love the deppressing vibe of the album and it's theme of addiction but my main gripe is that some songs are really mediocre and unfinished. My favourite songs are: Burn, Already Dead (my top 5 favourite songs ever), Cigarettes, Sometimes, Doom, Until The Plug Comes Back Around, From My Window, Feel Alone, My Life In A Nutshell, Rich And Blind(deluxe) and Legends(deluxe).

5. Kiss Land

Kiss Land is also a near perfect album. The story, vibe and atmosphere are some of my favourites. The main theme about the album is the tour life and how the artist deals with loneliness that comes with it. The sound of rain thounder and the sense of heartbreak and travelling are unforgettable. My main problem is that the 2 starting songs are mediocre compared to the rest of the album. My favourite songs are: Adaptation, Love In The Sky, Belong To The World (probably my second favourite song of all time), Live For, Wanderlust, Kiss Land, Pretty and Tears In The Rain.
